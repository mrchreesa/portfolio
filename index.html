🥗 Nutri-Tracker App

🖥LIVE SITE PREVIEW🖥

📜CODE PREVIEW📜

Nutri-Tracker is an app that allows you to search through 30,000 foods with pictures and their nutritions. After registration or login, you can start the search of your desired foods. By clicking on individual food you will see a dropdown menu with a graph showing all essential nutrients. Then you can choose to add the chosen food to you profile. A summary of all the nutritions and foods added in a given day can be found by accessing you profile from the icon in the top-left corner. Happy Nutri-Tracking!

🏡 AirBnB Clone

🖥LIVE SITE PREVIEW🖥

📜CODE PREVIEW📜

AirBnB Clone as the name suggests is a clone of the property rental site AirBnB. I've followed the original design to the best of my abilities as a reference. Navigating from the landing page you will see the properies list. All properties are being added throught the CMS of Sanity.io. After selecting a property of your choice, you will be taken to a page with all the necessary details of that property. There you can find a calendar to select the desired dates for your stay as well as a window which calculates the total cost. On the bottom of the page is Google Maps location of your property.
