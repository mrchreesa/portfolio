🥗 Nutri-Tracker App

🖥LIVE SITE PREVIEW🖥

📜CODE PREVIEW📜

Nutri-Tracker is an app that allows you to search through 30,000 foods with pictures and their nutritions. After registration or login, you can start the search of your desired foods. By clicking on individual food you will see a dropdown menu with a graph showing all essential nutrients. Then you can choose to add the chosen food to you profile. A summary of all the nutritions and foods added in a given day can be found by accessing you profile from the icon in the top-left corner. Happy Nutri-Tracking!
